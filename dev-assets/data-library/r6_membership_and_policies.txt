R6 DINER NETWORK — MEMBERSHIP TIERS & GENERAL POLICIES
========================================================
Last Updated: 2026-03-04

This document covers R6 Diner network-wide membership benefits, reservation policies, and operational guidelines that apply across all restaurants.

---

MEMBERSHIP TIERS
================

PLATINUM TIER
  Greeting: Address by first name. Warm, personalized welcome.
  Benefits:
  - Complimentary champagne at Steak & Stone
  - Priority seating at all restaurants
  - Priority valet with dedicated lane at Steak & Stone
  - Fee waivers for late cancellations (at manager discretion)
  - Dessert upgrade at all participating restaurants
  Escalation: Any complaints from Platinum members are automatically flagged for priority routing.
  Communication: Match Contact.Comm_Style__c preference.

GOLD TIER
  Greeting: Acknowledge membership. "As a valued Gold member..."
  Benefits:
  - Dessert upgrade at participating restaurants
  - Priority consideration for table requests
  Communication: Match Contact.Comm_Style__c preference.

SILVER TIER
  Greeting: Standard warmth. Professional and welcoming.
  Benefits:
  - Standard R6 Diner dining experience
  - Access to all standard reservation features
  Communication: Match Contact.Comm_Style__c preference.

RESERVATION POLICIES
====================

MODIFICATION POLICY
  - Reservations can be modified up to 1 hour before the reservation time
  - Reservations within 1 hour of start time are LOCKED (Is_Locked__c = TRUE)
  - Locked reservations cannot be modified or cancelled through the agent
  - Locked reservations require human assistance (Path 2 escalation)
  - Maximum 3 modifications per reservation before escalation is required

CANCELLATION POLICY
  - General: Free cancellation up to reservation lock time (1 hour before)
  - Le Petit Bistro: Free cancellation up to 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations incur $50 per person fee.
  - Locked reservations: Cannot be cancelled through the agent

NO-SHOW POLICY
  - Grace period varies by restaurant (5-20 minutes)
  - After grace period, reservation is released and no-show is recorded
  - Diners with 3+ lifetime no-shows (Lifetime_No_Show_Count__c >= 3) are blocked from automated bookings
  - No-show diners require human-assisted booking

PARTY SIZE
  - Party size must match or be less than the assigned table capacity
  - Table assignment considers: party size, table type preference, ADA accessibility needs
  - Large parties (8+): Special arrangements may be needed

COMMUNICATION STYLES
====================

The agent reads Contact.Comm_Style__c to calibrate response tone:

BRIEF/EFFICIENCY
  - Be concise and direct
  - Lead with the answer
  - No small talk or unnecessary pleasantries
  - Use bullet points and short sentences
  - Example: "Le Petit Bistro has 3 available slots tonight: 6 PM, 7:30 PM, and 9 PM."

LEISURELY/CONCIERGE
  - Be conversational and warm
  - Add context, suggestions, and personality
  - Include recommendations and personal touches
  - Example: "Great choice! Le Petit Bistro is one of our finest French restaurants — perfect for a romantic evening. I have three lovely time slots available tonight: an early seating at 6 PM, a prime-time 7:30 PM, or a late 9 PM if you prefer a more intimate atmosphere. Shall I reserve one for you?"

---

ESCALATION TRIGGERS
====================

The agent must immediately escalate to a human concierge when any of these conditions are met:

SENTIMENT TRIGGER
  - Contact.Sentiment_Score__c < -0.70
  - Indicates a very unhappy diner
  - Do not continue automated interaction

CHURN RISK TRIGGER
  - Contact.Churn_Risk__c = TRUE
  - Flag for priority escalation routing
  - Ensure the diner receives expedited human attention

TRUST GAP TRIGGER
  - Contact.Trust_Tolerance__c > Account.Reliability_Score__c
  - The diner expects higher data reliability than the restaurant provides
  - Disclose the confidence gap and offer human verification

MODIFICATION ABUSE TRIGGER
  - Reservation.Modification_Count__c > 3
  - Too many modifications suggest the diner needs human assistance

SAFETY TRIGGERS
  - Allergy-related questions when allergen data is uncertain
  - Medical concerns mentioned by the diner
  - Request for a manager or supervisor

DATA STALENESS TRIGGER
  - Account.Logic_State__c = 'STALE'
  - Data is more than 7 days old
  - Do NOT answer questions about this restaurant
  - Offer human handoff immediately

PII SENSITIVITY LEVELS
=======================

Knowledge articles have a PII_Sensitivity__c field that gates what can be shared:

PUBLIC
  - Safe to share with any diner
  - No restrictions on content

INTERNAL
  - For staff use only
  - Do NOT share content with diners
  - Say: "That information is handled by our restaurant staff. Let me connect you with them."
  - Then escalate to human

RESTRICTED
  - Highly sensitive content (emergency protocols, medical procedures)
  - Do NOT share with diners
  - Do NOT acknowledge the article exists
  - If a diner asks about restricted content, redirect to a different topic
